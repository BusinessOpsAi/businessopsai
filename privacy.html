Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

BusinessOps AI respects the privacy of visitors, prospects, and customers. This policy explains the basic information we may receive when you contact us, request a pilot review, ask a question, or use this website.

Information you provide

If you submit a form or send an email, you may provide your name, business name, email address, phone number, business type, selected plan, main pain point, question, and message details. This may include business inquiry information about your current lead response, follow-up, admin work, tools, or operational goals. Because this website is static, forms open your email app so you can review the message before sending it.

How information is used

Information you provide may be used to respond to questions, discuss pilot fit, scope potential workflows, provide support, improve service design, and communicate about BusinessOps AI services by email or phone.

Service and third-party tools

BusinessOps AI may use third-party tools to operate the website, manage communications, support pilot workflows, or provide requested services. Any use of third-party tools should be limited to what is reasonably needed for the service or inquiry.

Sharing

BusinessOps AI does not sell visitor contact information. Information may be shared only when needed to provide services, comply with legal obligations, or protect legitimate business interests.

Data security

BusinessOps AI aims to use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards appropriate for a small business service provider. No website, email system, or internet transmission can be guaranteed perfectly secure.

Your choices

You can choose not to submit website forms and instead contact BusinessOps AI directly by email or phone. You can also request updates or corrections to information you previously provided.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to businessopsai@gmail.com or by calling 209-400-5825.
